Interactive 2D vector board

Spline Lab

A first-pass sketchpad for the vehicle path-motion geometry. Pan and zoom the board, add free vectors, drag handles, and use the vehicle scaffold to reason in normalized coordinates where the wheelbase is fixed at 1 and the motion is controlled by the vehicle geometry inputs.

Mode: select
Geometry: normalized
Selected: none
Wheel drag: pan · Shift+wheel: zoom
Drag a vector handle to edit it. Drag empty space to pan. Double-click empty space to create a vector.
x: 0.00, y: 0.00

Inspector

The left side is the drawing surface. This panel edits the vehicle scaffold and any selected free vector. The vehicle overlay is drawn in normalized units with wheelbase fixed at 1.

Vehicle geometry
Radius is signed. Positive values turn left, negative values turn right. Curve length is the arc length traveled in wheelbase units. Sl drives the stamped series, while the root θ stays at 0.
